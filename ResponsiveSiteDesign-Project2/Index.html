About Vijayawada Airport

Vijayawada Airport is a public airport serving Andhra Pradesh Capital City Area , India. The airport is located 13.5 km (8.4 mi) from the Vijayawada city, on National Highway 5 . During the World War II , this airfield served as an army base , after it was converted into a civilian airport. Air Deccan introduced a Flight between Hyderabad and Vijayawada in 24 September 2003. The airport covers an area of 537 acres (217 ha) and the runway is 2,286 metres (7,500 ft) long. This Airport Is an Operational Hub For Aircosta.
